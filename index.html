About me

I am a Digital Marketer with sweet tooth for problem solving. In this day and age of ultra digital technology, not establishing a digital footprint is basically setting up for failure.

I can give you the website that instantly magnetizes your audience and assure you increasing sales by the day, be it a simple static webpage that shows off your product's flair or a backend webapp in Django with ML/AI capabilities.
